I am excited to begin a career in web development because:

  • It offers flexibility and the potential for remote work.
  • It is an exciting, dynamic field that will always offer me new things to learn and master.
  • I love troubleshooting and problem solving, and I find the logic of coding very fascinating.

About Me

Before becoming interested in web devlopment, I went to West Virginia University and got a dual bachelor's degree in Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering. I am now pursuing a career in web development to add to my skillset and gain more flexibility in my career.

One of my biggest passions in life is traveling and seeing new places. I am hoping that a career in web development will allow me the flexibility to focus more on that. My fiance and I are working on converting a van and hope to be able to live on the move for a few months at a time, traveling all over North America.

So far, I can provide services in basic front-end website design, including HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript. Over the next few months, I will be learning more advanced skills in these areas as well as learning some back-end development to expand my skillset. I am excited to continue to learn and develop these skills to become a great developer who can offer professional services across a full-stack project.
